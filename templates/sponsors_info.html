Why Sponsor TransparencyCamp?

Sunlight Foundation’s annual TransparencyCamp (TCamp) is the premier event for technologists, government officials, journalists, policy-makers, students and impassioned citizens from across the country and around the world to discuss and share new approaches, technologies and policy prescriptions to make government more open, transparent and accountable.

This two-day event is organized as an “unconference” and emphasizes the important contributions that each and every attendee can bring to the sessions, workshops, and conversations. In fact, attendees help determine the schedule for the conference and are encouraged to suggest and lead sessions themselves. Bringing together hundreds of creative innovators, leaders and change-makers, TransparencyCamp has been the spark for a lot of new ideas, collaborations and initiatives such as CityCamp, CrisisCommons, numerous local open government initiatives and international TransparencyCamps.

Your sponsorship helps support this unique gathering and demonstrates your commitment to open government to our motivated attendees. Check out our highlight video from TransparencyCamp 2011.
