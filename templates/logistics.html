The Deal

TCamp is a come-as-you-are environment. If that means jeans, sandals, ties, or pearls to you, then so be it. The rest of us will be casual (or possibly in bear suits). We care more about the substance of what you have to say than what you wear while saying it.

Eats

We provide you with coffee, breakfast, lunch, and snacks for both Saturday and Sunday, plus an awesome happy hour in the middle. Expect to eat well, whether you're an omnivore, herbivore or gluten-free-ivore.

Happy Hour!

A happy hour event is in the works for 2013. We will update you when we have details.

Where is TransparencyCamp?

The George Washington University, Marvin Center

800 21st St. NW, Washington, DC 20052

Plan Your Stay

TransparencyCamp 2013 at the Marvin Center is located close to the Foggy Bottom Metro stop on the Orange and Blue lines of the DC Metro. If you're planning on using public transportation to get to Camp, locations near the Blue/Orange line are ideal.

Parking

Street parking in Foggy Bottom is limited, so Metro, bus, cycling or moonwalking may be your best options. Still intent on driving? There are Some lots available to GW visitors for a fee.

When do I need to get there?

Registration and coffee will start at 9am on Saturday, April 30th. The Opening Session starts at 10am -- don't miss it!

What should I bring?

Please help us keep TransparencyCamp as green as possible by bringing a water bottle and/or coffee mug to help keep waste down. Power strips and chargers for your computer, phone, etc. are also never bad ideas. If you're planning to use a projector during a presentation, please bring your own VGA adaptor. Every room will have a projector, but we can't promise we'll have the right adaptors for your computer. If you need to purchase an adaptor, there is an Apple Store near the Bethesda Metro station and a Best Buy at the Tenleytown-AU Metro station.

What should I wear?

Check the Capitol Weather Gang blog for the latest weather conditions. Dress for the Camp is casual -- just be comfortable.

Will there be internet?

Yes, we'll have wireless provided. Cell phone reception inside the building isn't great, though.
