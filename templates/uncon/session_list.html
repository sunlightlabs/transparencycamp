Transparency Camp is an "unconference", which means that participants set the agenda, lead sessions, and drive the dialogue. Although we’ll give you opportunities to brainstorm and share ideas for sessions ahead of time, most of the schedule will be set during the event itself. So, when you attend, be sure to bring along some ideas for what you’d like to talk about!

To get a sense of what sessions and agenda from last year, check out the Transparency Camp 2011 schedule listed below. Look out for another like it for your ideas for TCamp 2012 soon!
